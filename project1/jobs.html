Position 2: Cybersecurity Analyst

Reference Number: CSA02

Salary Range: $90,000 - $130,000 per year

Reports To: IT Security Manager

Key Responsibilities:

  • Monitor and analyze network traffic for security threats.
  • Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.
  • Develop and implement security policies and protocols.
  • Respond to and investigate security incidents.

Required Qualifications:

  1. Strong understanding of cybersecurity principles and tools.
  2. 2+ years of experience in a cybersecurity role.
  3. Proficiency in tools like Wireshark, Nessus, and Splunk.

Essential:

  • Strong understanding of cybersecurity principles and tools.
  • 2+ years of experience in a cybersecurity role.
  • Proficiency in tools like Wireshark, Nessus, and Splunk.

Preferable:

  • Certifications such as CISSP, CEH, or CompTIA Security+.
  • Experience with incident response and forensic analysis.
